History

What is now Ecuador, formed part of the northern Inca Empire until the Spanish conquest in 1533.
Between 1904 and 1942, Ecuador lost territories in a series of conflicts with its neighbors.
Although Ecuador marked 30 years of civilian governance in 2004, the period was marred by political instability.
In late 2008, voters approved a new constitution, Ecuador's 20th since gaining independence.

Geography

Location

Ecuador is a country in western South America next to the Pacific Ocean, between Colombia and Peru and lays in the Equatorial line.

Terrain

Ecuador has 4 regions:

Coastal plain (costa).
Inter-Andean central highlands (sierra).
Eastern jungle (oriente).
Galapagos Islands (insular).

One of the most famous tourism places to go in Ecuador is Galapagos Islands.
